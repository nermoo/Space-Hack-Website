SpaceHack

SpaceHack 2021 is an idea hackathon that has been organized by the SEDS Sabra with the collaboration of the Students for the Exploration and Development of Space Sri Lanka (SEDS Sri Lanka) and with the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) Student Branch of the Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka. The hackathon is open for all the undergraduates of Sri Lanka. Participants will be exposed to Space Exploration and related fields and can be experienced in Earth Science and Space Exploration to make a better life on Earth through new scientific discoveries.

Challenge

In Sri Lanka, many space enthusiasts are willing to explore space by learning space exploration concepts. But for them, there are only fewer resources to study and most of them are very complex to perceive the space exploration theories and concepts. So the challenge for you is to come up with an outstanding solution for the above challenge that can give these space exploration theories and concepts simply and creatively to the community, in a way that everyone can easily understand.

Event Guidelines

The Contestants are required to comply with conditions of the contest rules and regulations,according to the below provisions.



  • Undergraduates of any University or Higher Educational Institute are eligible to participate in this competition.
  • A team must comprise 3 to 5 members
  • Participants in a team should represent the same University or the Higher Educational Institute
  • The proposed idea cannot be changed. The final product may have added features.
  • By registering for this contest, you hereby agree to receive results on the complication depending on the effort.
